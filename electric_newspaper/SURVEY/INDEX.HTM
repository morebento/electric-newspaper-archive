Ok, so you all know about us and we know that you like what we are doing by producing what "The Advertiser" and other media in Adelaide aren't, but we would like to know more about you.

That's why we have come up with this reader survey, to find out more about the people who visit and read this site.

Why would we want to do this? Well, mainly so we can find out what you like and dislike about the site, what suggestions you have, and general ideas to help us produce a better more informative publication.

All you need to do is fill in the details in the form below, and when you have finished hit the "send it to us" button at the bottom of the page. This will take you about 3-4 minutes

You will notice we are not asking for your names or personal information. We are also not going to divulge or divest this information to anyone else apart from ourselves. Trust us, we value our readership.
