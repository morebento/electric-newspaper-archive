Monday, April 6, 1998

From Celtic roots

The mid-north town of Kapunda, one of South Australia's oldest mining towns, celebrated its Celtic roots with a three-day festival which finished with a street parade through the town yesterday.

The Celtic ancestry of Kapunda is strongly rooted in the Cornish population which settled in the area in colonial times.
