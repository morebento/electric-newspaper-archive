photo-essay: Tania Liemareff

A sparkling blue day. Auto-suggest - drive. Drive where? Old Port Road,
15 minutes from the city.

Port Road was once planned to be the route for a canal; it is now one of Adelaide's widest roads. Century year-old palms and the tallest flagpole in South Australia loom large from water-guzzling green lawns on which no one ever sits or uses, dividing the dual carriageway and random blocks of deserted commercial conglomerates, pockets of abandoned narrow shops spilling tears of rusted iron and shards of glass onto weedy footpaths.
