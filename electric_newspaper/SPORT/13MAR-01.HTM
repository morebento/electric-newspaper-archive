Friday March 13, 1998

AAMI Thunderbirds announce netball captains

The Adelaide AAMI Thunderbirds Netball team has announced its captain and vice-captain for the coming national netball season, where they hope to go one better than in 1997.

Coach Marg Angove announced the Thunderbirds two Australian defenders, Kathryn Harby and Sarah Sutter, would again be captain and vice-captain respectively.

The announcement was made as the two defenders returned from their successful national tour of the Caribbean, along with centre-court specialist, Rebecca Sanders.

With only one week to go before the national netball series kicks off, Angove is looking forward to a full contingent at training next week.

"The past few weeks have been difficult, with three of our starting seven away with national commitments, and we are all looking forward to a strong week of training before the season begins next Friday night," she said.

While the Thunderbirds will play a demonstration match against the Adelaide Ravens this weekend to mark the official opening of ETSA Park, Angove said the team was focusing on its grand final re-match with Melbourne Phoenix next Friday.

The opening match for the Thunderbirds will be the first ever national league game at South Australia’s new netball stadium, ETSA Park, at Mile End.

And it promises to be a thriller, with the Thunderbirds taking on reigning premiers, Melbourne Phoenix at 7.30pm.
