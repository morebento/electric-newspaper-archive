Thursday March 5, 1998

Free Prix
by Piston Stoned in Melbourne

The Grand Prix season begins here on Sunday, and with today's arrival of Michael Schumacher all the drivers are now in town.

At a media conference at Albert Park this morning, Mr Schumacher said Melbourne wasn't his favourite circuit, but "not every circuit can be your favourite, obviously".

"There are only one or two corners where you can do much. Otherwise it's just braking and accelerating," he said of the Albert Park layout.

The Electric Newspaper has its own reporter and stills photographer at the Grand Prix, and naturally, our coverage will be free - and comprehensive.
