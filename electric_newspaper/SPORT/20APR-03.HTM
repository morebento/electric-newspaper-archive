Monday April 20, 1998

National Rugby League, Round 6, Results

Manly 28 (A Hunter 2 N Kosef S Menzies T Hill J Hopoate tries; C Field 2 goals) Brisbane 4 (A Langer try) at Brookvale Oval. Crowd: 13,194. Referee: S Clark.

Sydney City 50 (L Ricketson 2 N Wood 2 A Lam B Fittler M Sing R Barnett A Walker tries; I Cleary 7 goals) Adelaide 12 (D Grimley M Daylight tries; L Williamson 2 goals) at Sydney Football Stadium. Crowd: 6200. Referee: R Lawrence.

Illawarra 22 (B Reeves S Timmins C Fitzgibbon A Purcell tries; C Fitzgibbon 2 D Bradstreet goals) Canberra 20 (J Croker B Kennedy L Priddis tries; D Furner 4 goals) at Bruce Stadium. Crowd: 8238. Referee: S Hampstead.

Balmain 11 (L Milford M O'Neill tries; S Nevin goal, field goal) Cronulla 10 (R Richardson A Ettingshausen tries; M Rogers goal) at Shark Park. Crowd: 16,598. Referee: B Harrigan.

North Queensland 17 (K Warren 2 J Hannay tries; J Hannay 2 goals, A Dunnemann field goal) Newcastle 4 (J Moodie try) at Malanda Stadium, Townsville. Crowd: 18,428. Referee: T Mander.

St George 30 (M Coyne D Treacy J Ainscough L Thompson N Blacklock tries; W Bartrim 5 goals) Penrith 24 (P Adamson R Beckett P Jorgensen A Hinson tries; R Girdler 4 goals) at Penrith Stadium. Crowd: 12,227. Referee: P Simpkins.

North Sydney 44 (N Roy 2 J Taylor 2 M Soden A Muir M Buettner tries; J Taylor 8 goals) Auckland 26 (S Jones T Tapupu N Vagana A Lauitiiti tries; M Ridge 5 goals) at North Sydney Oval. Crowd: 12,000. Referee: P McBlane.

South Sydney 41 (J Schloss C Caruana T Brasher J O'Neill G Peats J Smith tries; J O'Neill 8 goals, D Trindall field goal) Western Suburbs 10 (D Willis A Lang tries; L Dynevor goal) at Sydney Football Stadium. Crowd: 6925. Referee: E Ward.

Melbourne 32 (R Ross M Bai B Kimmorley R Howe T Nikau J Carlaw tries; C Smith 4 goals) Parramatta 22 (P Carige D Penna J Simon tries; C Schifcofske 5 goals) at Parramatta Stadium. Crowd: 15,434. Referee: B Grant.

Canterbury 56 (S Hughes 2 D Halligan 2 G Fleming H El Masri C Polla-Mounter T Stone T Norton tries; D Halligan 10 goals) Gold Coast 10 (J Nicol P Campbell tries; G Mackay goal) at Belmore Sports Ground. Crowd: 5179. Referee: M Hewitt.
