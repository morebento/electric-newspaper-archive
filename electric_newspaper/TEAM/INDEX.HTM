Editor
Hendrik Gout has worked in Queensland, New South Wales, Victoria, and Tasmania on daily newspapers, radio, and television news and current affairs with commercial TV and the A.B.C. He was Executive Producer of the 7.30 Report before moving to Adelaide, where he covered the State Bank Royal Commission and politics. Mr Gout now runs his own media company.

Email: editor@electric.on.net
